INGRIDIENTS

  • 200 g noodles (I am using chow mein noodles)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 medium-sized onion, sliced
  • 1 large-sized red or green capsicum, (bell pepper) cut into ¼-inch strips
  • 1/2 cupcabbage, sliced
  • 1 medium-sized carrot, cut into strips
  • 2 tablespoons + 2 tablespoons spring onion greens
  • 1 teaspoon soya sauce
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon schezwan sauce (add more, if you want really spicy noodles)
  • Grond pepper to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Boil water along with 1/4 teaspoon salt in a pan.
  2. Add noodles and boil until cooked.
  3. Drain the noodles and add drizzle 1 teaspoon oil.
  4. Toss well and set aside.
  5. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a pan/wok.
  6. Add 1 tablespoon crushed ginger-garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  7. Add onions, carrots, beans, bell pepper, cabbage, and saute on high heat for 2-3 mins.
  8. Add white vinegar, soya sauce, schezwan sauce, and mix well.
  9. Now add the boiled noodles, spring onion greens, ground black pepper, and salt to taste.
  10. Toss everything well.
  11. Garnish Veg Schezwan Noodles with some spring onions and sesame seeds. Serve hot.
